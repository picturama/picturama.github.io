Features

  • Scan local files - Keep your privacy. Don't upload your private photos to a cloud service and give a big company the rights to analyze them and do whatever they like.
  • Non-Destructive - The original images won't be touched
    (unless you purge the trash)
  • Read various photo formats: JPG, PNG, TIF, WebP, HEIC / HEIF
  • Read raw formats of a whole bunch of cameras
    (only on Mac and Linux, see Issue #25)
  • Browse photos by dates
  • View photo in detail (zoomable)
  • View EXIF information
  • Tags
  • Favorites
  • Rotate, tilt and crop photos
  • Delete photos
